Welcome to Anxout

Anxout is a project that I started working in 2022, due to some things that happened in my life that year.
The main objective and purpose is to help people that usually suffer from anxiety, depression or
any mental issue, to talk to someone and make helping connections.
Basically it's a place where you can be yourself without feeling judged.
