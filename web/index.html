Welcome to Anxout

Anxout is a project/community that I thought about and start working on it in 2022, due to some things that happened in my life that year.
The main objective and purpouse is to help people (specially people that suffer from anxiety, depression or any mental issue), to talk to someone or make friends.
Basically it's a place where you can be yourself without feeling judged.
